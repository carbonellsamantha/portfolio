Smashley's Design Portfolio

I connect business goals with user needs and help teams create clear, purposeful work with lasting impact. I focus on growing designers, removing obstacles, and guiding projects toward confident, well-reasoned decisions.

My design philosophies are grounded in four primary ideas: craft, execution, team dynamics, and mindset.

Craft

Beauty is function.

I believe in the Aesthetic-Usability Effect—design without beauty lacks emotion. I'm an artist at heart, and I design for how things feel, not just how they work.

Execution

Release & refine.

Traditional UX steps still have their place, but they can slow progress when overused—so be intentional about when to use them, explore options faster with AI.

Team dynamics

Operations are key.

As AI makes it easier for anyone to ship products to users, operations matter more than ever—helping teams collaborate, stay aligned, make intentional decisions.

Mindset

Be curious & adapt.

12 years in the industry, one thing is clear—just when you think you've figured it out, everything changes. Stay curious, keep learning, and adapt.
